## Read `Gpt-5` prompt Guide and Prompt Engineering 
* To get The best output from Ai write  prompts in the following steps:
* `Role`: What and how much do you know about the question asked 
* `Task`:A short statement about what output do you want.
* `Context`:Now briefly describe what do you want 
* `Rules`:Various do's and dont's that you want
in your results.
* `Stop Conditions`:Tell it the condition at which stage your work is completed and want it to stop.
* `Format`:Tell AI the format in which you wnat the results. 
* Also write the prompts in a way which helps to get the better result instead of confusing the AI like writing some conflict statements.
